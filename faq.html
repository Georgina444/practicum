Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the internship?

The internship program spans a duration of one month, offering a concise yet immersive experience aimed at providing comprehensive insight into our operations and fostering hands-on learning opportunities for aspiring professionals. This month-long engagement aims to equip participants with valuable skills and industry exposure, aligning with our commitment to nurturing talent and promoting growth within the organization.

Is the internship paid?

The internship is an unpaid opportunity. However, exceptional performance during this period may lead to potential consideration for a position within our company.

What specific knowledge and project
involvement can I expect to gain during the internship?

During the internship, the learning experience is tailored to individual skill levels. You'll gain practical knowledge and hands-on exposure by engaging with real-time projects. The complexity of the tasks and challenges you'll tackle correlates with your existing knowledge, ensuring a valuable and growth-oriented experience.
